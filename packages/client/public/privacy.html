Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Durak Online (the "Service") handles information when you use our Discord Activity.

Information We Process

  • Basic gameplay/session data required to run multiplayer matches (room IDs, game state, player turn data).
  • Technical diagnostics (e.g., errors, connection status) to keep the Service stable.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage multiplayer game sessions.
  • To synchronize game state between players in real time.
  • To troubleshoot performance and reliability issues.

Data Retention

Gameplay session data is generally transient and tied to active game rooms. We do not intentionally retain personal data longer than necessary to operate the Service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may use infrastructure providers to host the Service, which process data only to provide hosting and networking functionality.

Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your region).

Your Choices

You can stop data processing at any time by discontinuing use of the Service and leaving active game sessions.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Service operator through the official Discord application profile or support contact listed with the app release.
