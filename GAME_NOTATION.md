# Durak Game Action Log Notation

This document explains the format of the `actionLog` generated by the Durak game server. The action log tracks all significant events in a match chronologically. It can be used by an AI to replay matches, understand player strategies, or analyze game statistics.

## General Format

Cards are denoted by their suit symbol (`♠`, `♥`, `♦`, `♣`) followed by their value (`6`, `7`, `8`, `9`, `10`, `J`, `Q`, `K`, `A`). 
Example: `♠6` (Six of Spades), `♥A` (Ace of Hearts).

Player identifiers are their unique `sessionId` values from the server.

## Event Types

Below are the types of events logged during a match and their structure:

### 1. Game Initialization

#### `ground huzur: [Card]`
Indicates the trump card ("huzur") that determines the trump suit for the match and is placed at the bottom of the deck.
**Example:** `ground huzur: ♥10`

#### `turn 0: [PlayerID]: [Card], [Card], ...`
Records the initial cards drawn by each player at the very beginning of the match (Turn 0).
**Example:** `turn 0: Player1: ♠6, ♦7, ♣9, ♥K, ♠A`

### 2. Player Actions

#### `[PlayerID] attacked: [Card], [Card], ...`
A player initiated an attack or added to an ongoing attack with one or more cards of the same primary value.
**Example:** `Player1 attacked: ♠7`

#### `[PlayerID] defended: [Card], [Card], ...`
A player successfully defended against an attack playing appropriate higher value cards or trump cards. 
**Example:** `Player3 defended: ♠10`

#### `[PlayerID] picked up: [Card], [Card], ...`
The defending player chose to pick up the cards on the table rather than defending (or could not defend). All cards on the table are added to their hand.
**Example:** `Player3 picked up: ♠7, ♠10, ♦8`

### 3. Automatic End-of-Round Actions

#### `[PlayerID] drew: [Card], [Card], ...`
After an attack and defense phase resolves (either by a successful defense that is passed, or by the defender picking up), players draw from the deck to replenish their hands to 6 cards. The attacker draws first, then supporters, then the defender (if they defended).
**Example:** `Player1 drew: ♣A`
